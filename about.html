I bring users into every step of the product design process to create delightful user experiences.

User Experience Designer

Research & Requirements

I collaborate with key stakeholders to identify problems and help align business goals to end user experiences. I connect with end users to help bring real-life data, research and insights into products.

UX & IA

I help conduct user research, interviews and testing in addition to building user personas for teams to use in the product design and development process. I map out user flows, construct sitemaps and wireflows to map out user experience.

Interaction & Visual Design

I start with low-fidelity wireframes and flows and iterate through high-fidelity mockups. Through visual design, I create the look and feel of the experience as well as help build interaction standards through pattern libraries and design languages that guide a consistent, unified experience. I build interactive design protypes that can be used to test with users and help guide business teams in the right direction.

User Testing

My role is to constantly be a guiding compass to ensuring the user is included in every step of our process. From research into design through implementation, I am a relentless advocate for ensuring our design process is centered fully around the user.

Front End Development

I can handcraft front-end code and collaborate with development teams to ensure that high-fidelity designs are implemented into code that takes the design vision and turns it into real, usable interactive experiences that delight users and help seamlessly and intuitively serve their needs.

Community & Leadership

I remain incredibly dedicated and passionate about building community and helping bridge the diversity gap in technology. In my free time, you can find me volunteering for organizations that focus on empowering + connecting women and underrepresented groups to technology and building communities for design.
