I bring user experience into every step of a process to help guide products to the best solution.

User Experience Designer

Research & Requirements

I collaborate with key stakeholders to identify problems and help align business goals to end user experiences. I love diving into any problem and taking time to truly understand the business to help identify pain points and ask the critical questions that help us refine our scope and requirements to align with business needs and end user goals. I connect with end users to help bring real-life data and personas to projects that I use to help craft requirements and UX research.

Information Architecture

I help build user personas and translate requirements into wireframes and lo-fidelity prototypes to help give an initial vision to the experience. I map out user flows and and sitemaps to illustrate the archicture and process of the user experience and help teams understand the experience landscape.

Interaction & Visual Design

I translate high-level requirements and wireframes into high-fidelity mockups to bring a vision to life. Through visual design, I can design the look and feel of the experience as well as help build interaction standards through pattern libraries that guide a consistent, beautiful experience. I use the mockups to build interactive protypes that can be used to help guide business teams and early stage testing so we can constantly communicate and validate on the experience we’re building.

User Testing

My role is to constantly be a guiding compass to ensuring the user is included in every step of our process. From research to testing to analytics and Agile product iteration, I am a relentless advocate for ensuring our design process is centered fully around the user.

Front End Development

I can handcraft front-end code and collaborate with development teams to ensure that high-fidelity designs are implemented into code that takes the design vision and turns it into real, usable interactive experiences that delight users and help seamlessly and intuitively serve their needs.
