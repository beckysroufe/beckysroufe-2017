Responsive WebsiteUX & UI Designer, Front End Developer

I worked with a local Cincinnati client as a User Experience consultant to provide their business website with a complete redesign. I collaborated with the client and internal teams to help them align their business needs with end users from each stage of the process from initial research, planning, design and development.

1Research & Planning

I worked with a small business client to help them shape the vision for a business website redesign. We worked together to determine ultimately who the website would be intended for to help us guide initial user proto-personas. With our users defined, we could then discuss goals for our end users and better define our business goals. I provided initial documentation regarding an overall website sitemap and user flows to help us define the scope of the website and align these to our end user goals.
